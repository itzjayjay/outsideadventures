Mission Trails

Welcome to my second Outside Adventures website! This semester I went to Mission Trails and talked with a volunteer worker. Not as good as talking to a park ranger, but he still knew a lot about the wildlife of Mission Trails. I asked him a few questions and he responded as best he could. I also got his signature saying that I was there, signatures are on the Info page


Wildlife

The wildlife at Mission Trails is pretty interesting. I'm not the type of person who loves the outdoors and is fascinated by every animal or plant that crosses my path. However, I did find a really interesting species of rat called a dusky-footed woodrat, otherwise known as a "packrat". Facts about them are on the Facts page.
